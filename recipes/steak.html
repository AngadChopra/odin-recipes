A synonym of excellence, when someone thinks of a luxurious meal the image of a steak is one of the first which pops to mind. Why? Simple, it is the embodiment of luxury. It is a timeless classic like a datejust rolex or a well fitted suit. However despite being a classic, it has ever evolved despite its simplicity. We will sousvide the steak while basting it.

ingredients

  • A ribeyeDUH
  • butter
  • thyme

steps

  1. Set up the sousvide
  2. season steak with salt, pepper and any other seasoning
  3. add the steak to the bag with aromatics
  4. Sousvide the steak at 49 degree celcius
  5. sear the steak with butter
  6. baste to form a good sear
  7. rest the steak
